The same code, with a strong semantic approach:

```html
<article>
    <header>
        <figure>
            <img src="blog-post-img.jpg" alt="Seinfeld, a tv show about nothing" />
        </figure>
        <h2>My first blog post</h2>
        <span>Posted on <time datetime="2023-04-21">April 21, 2023</time></span>
    </header>
    <div>
        <p>This is the first blog post I've ever written, not very interesting... <a href="#">Read more</a></p>
    </div>
    <footer>
        <small>Category: <a href="#">Blah blah</a></small>
    </footer>
</article>
```
